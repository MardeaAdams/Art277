About Us

The New Bible Christian Fellowship Ministry is a bible beliving church that help young people come close to God and also help dougth beliving Christian to strenghten their faith and help give them a clear understanding in the world teaching and understanding the bible.
The (NBCFM) New Bible Christian Fellowship Ministry was establish 2021 my some powerful man of God who were chosen by God to help bring fourth his children to his church. If you are new to the area, or have recently developed or renewed your faith, you might be looking for a church to join. please take a look around this Website but also come and visit us soon. If you come to one of our sunday services, please introduce yourself to one of our welcome team, Bro. Philiph or Bro. Aaron. We will be pleased to answer any questions you have in person.

There are lots of ways to get to know people at New Bible Christian Fellowship Ministry. We have a range of smaller groups to help people make good friendships. You may want to join a home group to learn more about God through the Bible, discussion and prayer. You may want to join a group which enables you to enjoy the company of others who share a similar interest or stage in life. You can also find out about what our FUN Committee do and why they organise social events for us.

Whoever you are and whatever your interest in New Bible Christian Fellowship Ministry, we hope you find lots of useful information and features on this Website. If you can't find what you are looking for, please contact us using the contact details or email from on the contact us page.
